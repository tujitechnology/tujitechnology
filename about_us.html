Testimonials

Tujitechnologies Ltd.

Tujitechnologies is a young technology company in the field of Mobile Value Added Services (MVAS), Founded in 2015. We aim to add value to the Telecom Service Provider's business and operational environment and help create customer delight by delivering innovative products and services.

Being a true innovator in the mobility domain, TujiTechnology focuses on creating solutions that make mobile interactions a "Wanting Experience".
We are a New Entrant in a dynamic and rapidly growing Industry. Our business monetizes opportunities in converging services from global internet giants with telecom carriers across the globe.

We started working with some of the largest and most innovative mobile operators around the world - creating a global platform for discovery, access and monetization of the social internet. Tujitechnologies is managed by a team of entrepreneurs who has excelled in running numerous successful companies in mobile, internet and IT. The aim is to leverage the best practices from all three domains to offer the most comprehensive solution. Our approach is simple. We are extremely consumer focused and data driven with a passionate emphasis on improving the end-user experience.
